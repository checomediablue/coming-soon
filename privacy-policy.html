Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Checo Media Blue ("we", "us") is the data controller for personal data collected through this website. We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) in the United Kingdom.

1. What data we collect

  • Contact enquiries: name and email address when you submit our contact form.
  • Newsletter: email address when you subscribe via MailerLite.
  • Analytics: usage data (pages visited, browser type, IP address) via Microsoft Clarity, collected only with your consent.
  • Social feeds: publicly available social media content displayed via Curator.io, loaded only with your consent.

2. Lawful basis for processing

  • Consent – analytics and social feed cookies (you can withdraw via your browser or by declining our cookie banner).
  • Legitimate interests – responding to contact enquiries.
  • Contract performance – delivering the newsletter you signed up for.

3. How we use your data

  • To respond to enquiries and collaboration requests.
  • To send the weekly newsletter (you may unsubscribe at any time).
  • To understand how visitors use our site and improve it.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies for analytics (Microsoft Clarity) and social content (Curator.io). These are only activated after you give consent via our cookie banner. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies, which will cause the banner to appear again on your next visit.

5. Data retention

  • Contact form submissions: retained for up to 2 years, then deleted.
  • Newsletter subscribers: retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Analytics data: subject to Microsoft Clarity's own retention policy (up to 13 months).

6. Sharing your data

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted processors under strict data processing agreements:

  • MailerLite – newsletter delivery (EU/US Privacy Framework)
  • Microsoft Clarity – analytics (consent required)
  • Curator.io – social feed aggregation (consent required)
  • Formspree – contact form processing

7. Third-party links

Our website links to third-party platforms (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok etc.). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their own policies.

8. Children's privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. You also have the right to data portability and to object to processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO.
