Ethics & Collaboration Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Thank you for your interest in collaborating with me!

My name is Sandro Pages Scott, Founder of Checo Media Blue and Creator of I’m Checo Tech. Over the past few years, I’ve built a global audience across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and Threads, with millions of views and a highly engaged tech community.

I value honesty, transparency, and creative independence. This policy explains how I work with brands, and what you can expect when partnering with me.

Sponsored Content & Product Reviews

  • I collaborate only with products or brands I’ve personally tested or genuinely trust.
  • All gifted products or paid partnerships are fully disclosed in content and descriptions.
  • No free products alone will receive a review; our opinions remain unbiased.
  • Sponsored content includes YouTube’s “Includes Paid Promotion” notice where required.

Content Ownership & Creative Control

  • I produce, film, edit, and publish all content myself, retaining full creative control.
  • Brands may request one round of factual or visual corrections before final publication.

Usage Rights & Licensing

  • Brands can share content on social media with proper credit and a direct link.
  • Any use of content in paid ads or marketing campaigns requires a separate agreement.

Audience Engagement & Ethics

  • I do not buy followers, views, likes, or comments. All engagement is organic.
  • Average performance metrics are available via my Media Kit.

Scheduling & Lead Times

  • At least 14 days from product receipt are required to create content.
  • Publishing schedules are managed independently unless otherwise agreed in a contract.

Diversity & Inclusion

  • I actively support diverse campaigns and ensure balanced representation in all collaborations.
