About

Checo Media Blue is an independent UK-based media production company founded by Sandro Pages, the creator behind the tech and lifestyle platform I’m Checo Tech. Built from a passion for technology, creative storytelling and visual media, Checo Media Blue specialises in producing high-quality content for brands, creators and businesses looking to connect with modern digital audiences. The company handles everything from YouTube content and product reviews to social media campaigns, podcast production and branded video projects. It also serves as the official production house for the I’m Checo Tech channel, supporting the growth of the brand across platforms.
