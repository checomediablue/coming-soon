About Checo Media Blue

CheCo Media Blue is an independent, UK‑based media production company founded by Sandro Pages.

Focused on high-quality video, editorial and social-first content for the technology space — from in‑depth reviews and launch coverage to branded storytelling and campaign production.

The company also serves as the official production house for I'm Checo Tech, supporting long‑form YouTube content and short-form social media with a consistent creative standard.

At its core, Checo Media Blue exists to turn complex technology into accessible, trustworthy media designed for modern digital audiences.
