About Checo Media Blue

Checo Media Blue is an independent, UK‑based media production company founded by Sandro Pages.

Built around a clear focus on media, the company specialises in producing high‑quality video, editorial, and social‑first content for the technology space — from in‑depth reviews and launch coverage to branded storytelling and campaign production.

Working closely with global tech brands, Checo Media Blue prioritises clean visuals, performance‑driven testing, and transparent communication, ensuring every project delivers real value to both audiences and partners.

The company also serves as the official production house for I’m Checo Tech, supporting long‑form YouTube content, short‑form social media, and cross‑platform growth with a consistent creative and production standard.

At its core, Checo Media Blue exists to turn complex technology into accessible, trustworthy media — designed for modern digital audiences.
